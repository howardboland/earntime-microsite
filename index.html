How it works

Earn, spend, lock — repeat.

1

Earn

Pick a subject and difficulty, answer 5 AI-generated questions. A perfect Hard score earns 8 minutes; Easy earns 3 minutes.

2

Spend

Tap "Use Screen Time" to start a countdown timer. Kids always know exactly how much time they have left, right down to the second.

3

Lock

When time's up, the device locks. No more apps until they earn more — a full system overlay on Android, Guided Access on iOS.

Subjects

Six subjects, three age bands.

Questions are generated by Claude AI and calibrated to your child's age and chosen difficulty. Cached globally so the same great question set is shared across all users.
